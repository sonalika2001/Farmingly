THE PROCESS:
FROM THE FARM, WITHOUT THE HARM.

Step One

Spreading our ideas through workshops in rural farming districts, while keeping informative and short seminars for the farmers at our hubs, this will help them understand the benefits of our organisation, and help them understand how they might have been vulnerable in the past.

Step Two

Once the farmer is convinced to set up with us, we analyse their yield, and help them set an achievable target to deliver to us, this is done in a manner where the farmer is comfortable and there is no stressful deadlines.

Step Three

Necessary equipment like: good quality seeds, fertilizers and manures can be bought after consulting Farmingly employees at the hubs we have set up for this purpose, here the farmers will be asked to give utmost care to the crops while maintaining a standardized quality.

Step Four

Cycles of surveys will be done by the on-field experts to ensure:
1)All quality requirements are being met
2)There is no misconduct, during the entire process from sowing to harvest
3)To ensure there is no illegal methods being used to maximize yield, i.e. child labour, genetically modified crops(which are not part of the Farmingly standard)

Step Five

Post harvest the yield is compared with the targets, leverage is given in special scenarios (natural disasters, bad yield). The farmers are given the agreed amount of money and the yield is noted down in the logistical entries.

Step Six

The yield is now logistically tracked until it is distributed to the local vendors, they are branded separately for the consumers to recognize.
