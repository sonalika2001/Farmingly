Farmingly is an independent organisation empowering fair trading practices.

We exist to reform the system.

WHO we are

Noble & Public-Spirited

We distribute fruits and vegetables directly from the farm to wholesale buyers (supermarkets and vegetable vendors), removing the need for middlemen and agents who exploit farmers (in developing countries) due to illiteracy and unawareness.

WHAT we do

Mission & Vision

We aim to replace the system, which significantly affects the financially lower class of society by depriving them of fair pay for their labour.
Our goal is to create dominance in the market with an image of healthy buying.
We strive on keeping low-profit margins, or rather fair ones, to ensure the quality of the product and the financial well-being of the lower economic classes of these countries.

WHY we exist

For the right cause.

To provide better for both: consumer and producer. The producer demands fair pay for their yields and the consumer demands better quality of the product for the prices that they buy at.
